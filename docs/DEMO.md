# TikTok app-review demo script

Record one uninterrupted desktop capture in the TikTok Developer sandbox.

1. Open **TikTok Desktop Publisher** from the desktop.
2. Start on the creator-facing **Account** screen. Do not open the operator-only Settings tab during the demo.
3. Select **Connect with TikTok**.
4. Show TikTok Login Kit in the browser, account login, requested scopes, and consent.
5. Return to the app and show the creator nickname, username, privacy options, interaction restrictions, and maximum duration returned by `creator_info/query`.
6. Open the **Publish** screen.
7. Select a short MP4 created for the demo.
8. Enter a caption, choose `SELF_ONLY`, choose interaction settings, and tick the explicit authorization checkbox.
9. Select **Publish now**.
10. Show upload progress and the final publish status in the app.
11. Open TikTok and show the private post on the authorized creator account.
12. Optionally demonstrate **Schedule locally** and the **Local queue** tab.

Do not edit the recording to hide missing steps. Do not display access tokens, refresh tokens, or the client secret.
