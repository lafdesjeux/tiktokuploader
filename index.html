Creator-controlled video publishing from the desktop.

An open-source, reusable desktop interface for TikTok Login Kit and Content Posting API. Every creator authorizes their own account, reviews the video, selects the available privacy controls, and explicitly approves the export.

View source Installation

OAuth for every creator

Desktop Login Kit with PKCE and anti-CSRF state. No account or token is embedded in the application.

Current creator controls

The export screen queries creator information and displays the privacy and interaction options currently allowed by TikTok.

Local-first security

Videos and queue data remain on the creator's device. Tokens use the operating-system keyring when available.

Official Direct Post flow

Initialize the post, transfer the media in compliant chunks, and check asynchronous processing status.

Explicit export consent

Publishing is blocked until the creator confirms they reviewed and authorized the selected video.

Reusable automation

A compatible CLI and local queue support scripts, cron, launchd, and Windows Task Scheduler without exposing credentials.

End-to-end flow

  1. Connect a TikTok account
  2. Load creator permissions
  3. Review and approve the video
  4. Upload and track status
